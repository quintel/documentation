### New features

* The <a href="https://energytransitionmodel.com/scenario/demand/buildings/appliances" target="_blank">applications</a> section within the Buildings sector has been redesigned to provide more flexibility in the use of energy carriers.
* A new download has been added to the <a href="https://energytransitionmodel.com/scenario/data/data_export/yearly-energy-flows" target="_blank">Yearly energy flows</a> section, showing the yearly energy flows for the present year of the dataset.

### Bug fixes

* Some transport technologies were not taken into account in the <a href="https://energytransitionmodel.com/scenario/costs/specs_transport/efficiencies" target="_blank">transport efficiency</a> inputs. This has now been corrected.
* The exported crude oil flows were incorrectly shown in the 'Liquid fuels and feedstocks' chart. This has now been corrected.
